    Game Rules Summary

    • Pieces move 1 square orthogonally.
    • Rank: Elephant > Lion > Tiger > Leopard > Wolf > Dog > Cat > Rat
    • Higher rank captures lower. Equal ranks capture.
    • Exception: Rat captures Elephant (if Rat is on land).
    • Exception: Elephant cannot capture Rat.
    • Traps (Pink): Any piece entering an opponent's trap has its rank reduced to 0 (captured by anything). Own trap has no effect.
    • Water (Blue): Only Rat can enter. Cannot capture from water onto land (except Rat vs Rat).
    • River Jump: Lion and Tiger can jump vertically over river if no Rat blocks the way. Lion can also jump horizontally if no Rat blocks the way. Jump captures piece of equal/lower rank on landing square.
    • Dens (Marked): Win by entering opponent's Den. Cannot enter own Den. Traps near own Den are "friendly", traps near opponent Den are opponent's.
    • Win: Enter opponent Den or capture all opponent pieces.
